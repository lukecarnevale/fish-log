## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when using the App:

- **Account Information:** First name, last name, email address, phone number, date of birth, ZIP code
- **Profile Information:** Profile photo, NC fishing license status, WRC ID/Customer ID
- **Fishing Activity Data:** Species caught, fish counts, fish lengths, catch locations, fishing dates, photos of catches
- **Rewards Program Data:** Email for rewards program participation, entry history

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App features accessed, time spent in app, interaction patterns
- **Location Data:** Geographic location (with your permission) to record fishing locations

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

- **Authentication Providers:** When you sign in via email magic link
- **Cloud Services:** Data stored in our cloud database provider (Supabase)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Services:** Enable harvest reporting to NC Division of Marine Fisheries (DMF)
- **Manage Your Account:** Create and maintain your user profile
- **Rewards Program:** Administer the quarterly rewards drawing program
- **Improve the App:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality
- **Community Features:** Display catches in the community feed (with your consent)
- **Communications:** Send rewards notifications and important updates
- **Legal Compliance:** Meet regulatory requirements for harvest reporting
- **Security:** Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
